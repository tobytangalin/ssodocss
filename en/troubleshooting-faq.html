Deleting an enabled SSO connection

When you delete an enabled SSO connection:

  • Team members associated with the deleted SSO connection can still log in using the default Sitecore authentication. Before they can log in, they must reset their password.

Invitations

When you enable an SSO connection:

  • Pending invitations can still be accepted by email addresses not associated with the enabled SSO connection's domain.

  • Pending invitations to email addresses associated with the enabled SSO connection domain must be sent again.

  • New invitations can be sent only to email addresses that belong to enabled SSO connections' domains.

If you do not have an enabled SSO connection, you can send invitations to any and all email domains.
